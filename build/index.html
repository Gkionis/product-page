Take and process payments for your service

GOV.UK Pay makes it quicker and easier for service teams to process payments online. It’s easy to integrate with and gives users a trusted GOV.UK branded payment experience.

Benefits of GOV.UK Pay

Service teams using GOV.UK Pay can:

  • automate payments reconciliation and refunds
  • easily handle payment queries
  • email receipts to users
  • manage accepted debit and credit card types more conveniently
  • generate financial reports for a range of dates
  • easily switch payment service providers

Teams building the service can:

  • integrate with a well documented, modern API
  • use GOV.UK branded pages
  • work with a multidisciplinary team at the Government Digital Service (GDS)

Users paying for a government service will get:

  • a more consistent payment experience across government
  • a choice of how to pay
  • refunds more quickly
  • payment pages designed to be accessible and easy to use

How it works

GOV.UK Pay integrates with multiple payment service providers so that service teams don’t have to. This means that switching suppliers or activating new payment methods won’t require additional technical work for your team.

Integrating GOV.UK Pay with your service

GOV.UK Pay provides technical documentation to help your service:

  • use the API
  • integrate your service with GOV.UK Pay
  • run tests with the code
  • check code structures

Who’s using GOV.UK Pay

Central government organisations currently using GOV.UK Pay are:

  • Ministry of Justice
  • Office of the Public Guardian
  • Companies House
  • Home Office

GOV.UK Pay is not yet available to local authorities.
